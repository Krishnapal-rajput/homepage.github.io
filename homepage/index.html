This is CS50

CS50 (Computer Science 50) is an on-campus and online introductory course on computer science taught at Harvard University, Yale University and Dartmouth College.

CS50: Computer Science Courses and Programs from Harvard Introduction to Computer Science from Harvard, better known as CS50, is the largest course on the Harvard campus and more than 2,000,000 learners worldwide have registered for the course on edX.
